About Me

Hello! I'm Raja Roy, a dedicated Full-Stack Developer with a strong foundation in building dynamic and responsive web applications. My expertise spans across both front-end and back-end technologies, allowing me to craft complete and robust solutions from concept to deployment.

My journey in web development began with a curiosity for how digital experiences come to life, which quickly evolved into a passion for coding and problem-solving. I thrive on turning complex ideas into elegant, user-friendly interfaces and efficient, scalable server-side logic.

I am proficient in a diverse range of technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React for the front-end. On the back-end, I work with Node.js and PHP, coupled with databases like MySQL and SQL. My development workflow is streamlined by Git and Bash, and I leverage frameworks like Bootstrap and Tailwind CSS for rapid and responsive UI development.

I'm constantly exploring new tools and methodologies to enhance my skills and stay at the forefront of web development trends. I'm always eager to collaborate on innovative projects and contribute to a team that values clean code, continuous learning, and impactful solutions.
